Mythical Creatures

A simplified liquid / fluid container shader, made in stingray engine and its shader graph. Including a dirty glass shader. Due to limitations this is made entirely in shaders Im not using a custom clipping plane, nor any rigs with jiggle constraints. Below you can see the first pass as well as the final solution. This task was an early research on what we could do with the current limitations, and it was left to be iterated and developed further
